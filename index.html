ABOUT

the best or nothing

Khesari Lal Yadav is an Indian actor, singer and model associated with Bhojpuri cinema. His breakthrough came from in the form of 2012 film Saajan Chale Sasural. In 2019, he participated in the reality show Bigg Boss 13 as a contestant.

Early life and family

how everything began

Khesari Lal Yadav was born on 15 March 1986 in Siwan district, Bihar.[1] He is married to Chanda Devi. They have a son and a daughter.

Film Award

talented artists

He was awarded Best Popular Actor at the Bhojpuri Film Awards 2016, and UP Ratan award in 2017.In 2019, he got "Best Actor Award" In Sabrang Film Awards and Bhojpuri Cinema Screen and Stage Awards for 2018 Superhit film Sangharsh.

Life Journey

From the very beginning, Khesari Lal is a folk singer as well as a good dancer. In the beginning, he had to face many difficulties, it was very important to have money to become a Bhojpuri singer. He had to sell litti chokha to collect money, for this he left his big job in the force. A few years later he started singing in the Bhojpuri album. Due to which he got immense love for his songs in Uttar Pradesh, Bihar and Jharkhand and where Bhojpuri is spoken. He sang hundreds of Bhojpuri hit songs Piyawa Gaye Re Hamar Saudi Re Bhauji, Saiyan Arab Gayle Na and Saiyan Aiba Ki Na Aiba and Lehnga Meter is his famous album song.
